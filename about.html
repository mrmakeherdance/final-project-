About Me

I was born and raised in Miami, Florida. Growing up in a city full of culture and music, I was always surrounded by different sounds and influences. That environment played a big role in shaping my interest in music.

I first started playing guitar at the age of 10. At the time, it was something new and exciting, and I enjoyed learning the basics and experimenting with different sounds. However, after a few years, I ended up stepping away from playing for a while.

Eventually, I found my way back to guitar. Picking it up again felt natural, and I quickly regained my passion for playing. Since then, I’ve continued to improve, explore new styles, and grow as a musician.

Today, I still play guitar and continue to develop my skills. It’s something I genuinely enjoy and plan to keep doing as part of my everyday life.

My Interests in Music

  • Playing guitar and learning new techniques
  • Listening to different genres of music
  • Exploring new artists and sounds
  • Improving my creativity through music
