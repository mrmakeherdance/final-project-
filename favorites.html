My Inspirations

This page highlights some of the artists, bands, and sounds that inspire me as a guitarist. The music I listen to plays a big role in how I think about guitar, creativity, and personal expression.

My Favorite Inspirations

  1. Narrow Head
  2. Alice In Chains
  3. Jimi Hendrix
  4. King Krule
  5. My Bloody Valentine

Artists and Influence Table

Artist/Band Genre/Style Why They Inspire Me
Narrow Head Alternative Rock / Shoegaze I like their heavy guitar sound and emotional atmosphere.
Alice In Chains Grunge / Rock Their dark riffs and unique melodies stand out to me.
Jimi Hendrix Rock / Blues Rock His playing style, tone, and stage presence are legendary.
King Krule Indie / Experimental I enjoy the raw and creative style in his music.
My Bloody Valentine Shoegaze The layered guitar textures are inspiring to me.

My Favorite Performance

This video captures a live performance by the band Narrow Head at Gramps in Miami. It was one of the final shows held at the venue before it stopped hosting live performances, which makes it even more memorable to me. The energy of the crowd and the sound of the guitars in this setting really reflect the kind of music that inspires my own playing.
