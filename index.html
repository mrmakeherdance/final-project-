Introduction

Welcome to my website. This site shares my guitar journey, musical interests, personal experience, and the artists and sounds that inspire me. It was created as my final website project and reflects both my interest in music and the web design skills I learned in class.

What This Site Is About

This website gives an overview of who I am and what I enjoy. It includes information about my background, a gallery of images, a page of favorites with media content, and a contact page with a form for visitors to reach out.

My goal for this project is to build a website that is clean, easy to navigate, and reflective of the web design principles learned in class.
